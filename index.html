Happy Birthday, Juhi ❤️

It’s been almost 2 years since I’ve known you now… and honestly, I still don’t know how time passed this fast. It was crazzy.

We started so randomly — just sharing dog memes on Instagram 😄 And I still remember you saying we should probably do more than just send dog videos… and I’m really glad we did that.

Our first date was honestly such a bad place (the burnt Pumpkin Spice) 😂 But somehow that’s exactly what made it memorable. It became our kind of story. Then came the second, the third… and slowly it turned into late nights, early mornings, and all those conversations during commutes that I still think about.

I think somewhere along the way, I was already falling for you… but our Thailand trip together really sealed it for me.

I know I don’t always say things properly. And maybe i am not the best at expressing myself… but I really hope you feel it in the way I am around you, because I genuinely love being with you.

Tumhare saath ek comfort hai… it can never be measured. It just feels easy. It just feels right.

I’ll hopefully keep getting better with time — at understanding you more, and at saying things the way I should. But thank you, seriously, for bearing with me through all of this time.

I honestly can’t imagine my life any other way now.

There’s so much I admire about you — your generosity, the way you love me, and the way you’ve taken me in and made adjustments so naturally.

One of the best feelings for me now is just knowing that I get to come home… and you’re there.

Also haan… “maar padingla” main shayad thoda zyada use karta hoon 😄 (jk: Not enough)
